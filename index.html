World Equestrian Center Winter Spectacular — The New Force in Ocala

Since opening in 2021, World Equestrian Center has transformed the Ocala equestrian rental market — creating significant new demand for properties near the WEC campus on NW 80th Avenue. The WEC Winter Spectacular runs 12 weeks from December 31 through March 22, overlapping with the WEF season in Wellington and providing a world-class alternative circuit for riders who prefer Ocala's setting and facilities. Properties within a short drive of WEC command premium seasonal rental rates during the winter circuit season.

Ocala Year-Round Rental Activity

Unlike Wellington, which operates primarily as a seasonal market, Ocala's equestrian rental market is active year-round. HITS Ocala hosts major competitions throughout the year at Post Time Farm, and Ocala's extensive training facilities generate consistent demand for barn leases and stall rentals from trainers and riders based in the area permanently or for extended periods. Elite Equine Marketplace lists year-round Ocala barn leases alongside seasonal arrangements.
